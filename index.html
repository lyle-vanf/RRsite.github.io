About Realm Reckers

Realm Reckers is a "realplay" (aka. heavily edited) 5e Dungeons and Dragons podcast put together by four friends in their free time. This podcast features no professional voice acting, no prior podcasting experience and relys only on the best efforts of four buddies that don't really know what they're doing... but they're trying their best.

Join us in the adventures of Va'ant Gristel, Plutark Ramsey, and Atlas (yeah it's just Atlas) as they traverse the perils of the realms, listen in suspense as they face tragety, horror, and downright idiocy in their quest to become the selfish mercinaries they were always meant to be!
